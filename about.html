Built on Experience, Driven by Innovation

Coreshift Technologies Inc is a Calgary-based DevOps consultancy with a clear mission: to help organizations of all sizes harness the power of modern DevOps practices. With years of hands-on experience in infrastructure engineering and cloud architecture, we bring enterprise-grade solutions to businesses ready to scale.

We believe that every organization deserves access to robust, scalable infrastructure. Whether you're a startup looking to build your first CI/CD pipeline or an established company modernizing your cloud infrastructure, we're here to help you succeed.
